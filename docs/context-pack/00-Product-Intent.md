# Product intent

## The product

MindWorks, currently delivered through the Helio therapist workspace, is a paid subscription clinical workspace for independent psychotherapists and small private practices.

It reduces the cognitive and administrative load surrounding therapy. It helps a therapist prepare, remain present, review sessions, organise approved source material, and recover continuity over time.

The recurring value is operational and clinical support in the therapist's normal working rhythm: every client, session, note, preparation and review cycle.

## The commercial hypothesis

Therapists will pay for a calm, trustworthy workspace that saves time, reduces cognitive burden, and supports continuity without undermining professional judgement or the therapeutic relationship.

The first product tests are whether therapists experience:

1. Less time spent on session-related administration.
2. Less effort recovering context between sessions.
3. More capacity to attend to clients during the therapeutic relationship.

## What it is not

- Not an EHR, CRM, generic task manager, or standalone messaging product.
- Not a system that replaces therapist thinking, interpretation, judgement, or relationship.
- Not a diagnostic, risk-scoring, or autonomous clinical-decision system.
- Not a consumer discovery or behavioural-investigation product.
- Not a product whose value depends on claiming that AI produces psychological insight.

## AI's proper role

AI may notice, structure and draft when a therapist asks it to. Any pattern support is provisional, session-specific, reviewable, and subordinate to therapist judgement. It must never be represented as a clinical conclusion or become part of a clinical record automatically.

Continuity helps the therapist re-enter approved prior work without rereading everything or relying entirely on memory. Its value is reduced effort and stronger orientation—not supposedly deeper automated discoveries.

> Patterns and insight do not change people. Therapists do.
